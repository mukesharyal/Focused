Data Collection and Usage
Our app does not collect, store, or track any user data. All streaks data is encrypted and stored locally on your device. We do not upload or process any personal information on external servers or cloud storage.
Third-Party Services
Focused uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect user data to provide personalized ads. Please review Google AdMob's Privacy Policy for more details on their data handling practices.
Accessibility Services
Our app utilizes Accessibility Services to monitor apps and websites in real-time for the purpose of blocking distractions. This functionality operates entirely on your device and does not log, store, or transmit any information externally.
Installed packages
Our app uses the PackageManager API to query installed apps so that they can be blocked, if needed. Focused does not log, store, or transmit any information externally.
Security of Data and Services
We prioritize the security of your data. Since no data is collected or stored externally, the risk of breaches or misuse is minimized.
