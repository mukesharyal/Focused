FAQs

What is Accessibility Service?
The Accessibility Service is a feature provided by Android. The Focused app relies on this technology to provide the blocking feature for apps and websites.
Do I need to turn it on all the time?
No. You can only turn it on when you use the Focused Mode.
Is my app activity monitored?
No. Focused doesn't track or store any of your data. The streaks data is also encrypted and stored in your device locally.
How does the app earn money?
The sole source of income for the app is the ad you see before you can turn Focused Mode on.
